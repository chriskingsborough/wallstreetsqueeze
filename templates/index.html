Wall Street Squeeze

How did you find your last stock? Our guess, if you're honest with yourself, is it was probably by accident. Maybe a friend suggested it, maybe you heard it in passing on a podcast, or maybe even on Reddit. At Wall Street Squeeze, we think you can do better and we're here to help.

We're here to help you find better stock. We provide collections of stocks based on fundamentals and momentum indicators. So next time you're ready to make an investment decision, start with the data, not a tip from your friend from college.

Collection Description
Indexes Contains standard indexes including the S&P 500, Dow Jones and Nasdaq 100.
Fundamental Contains collections based on fundamental indicators such as dividend rate and short interest.
Momentum The momentum collections identify stocks that have strong upwards or downwards momentum. The momentum collections identify stocks both above or below their moving averages and stocks near either their 52 week highs or lows.
Value The value collections are centered around stocks which present good "value" investments. This includes common metrics used by value investors, such as price to book ratio, price to earnings ratio and PEG ratio.
Specialty The specialty collection screens for stocks in specific industries. These special collections include screens for stocks for stocks associated with cryptocurrencies and blockchain, electric vehicles, online and video games, marijuana and cannabis, and pets.
